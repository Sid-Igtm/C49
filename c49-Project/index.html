Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • They can change the colour of their skin, which is important for their communication and also so that they can escape from predators.
  • They have a prehensile tale, which they use to wrap around tree branches for support, when trying to catch prey.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. They are known for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons were actually used for races and to deliver mails in the olden days.
